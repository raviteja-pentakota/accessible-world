Empowering Independence: Sighted Assistance Services

Providing compassionate and reliable sighted assistance for the visually impaired and elderly.

Our Mission

At Accessible World, we are committed to enhancing independence and mobility. Our Sighted Assistance Services offer professional and trustworthy support for daily activities, ensuring comfort, dignity, and confidence for every individual we serve.

Comprehensive Services

Our trained assistants are available to help with a wide range of needs, including:

  • Travel & Transportation: Navigating local and intercity travel, including assistance at railway stations, bus stations, and airports.
  • Personal & Official Tasks: Support with daily errands, shopping, and official documentation.
  • Mobility Support: Accompaniment during walks, visits to parks, and other outings.
  • Academic Assistance: Providing dedicated scribe services for visually impaired students during examinations.

A Partner in Your Journey

For instance, if you wish to explore the Zoo Park in Visakhapatnam, our assistant will be your dedicated companion, guiding you through the paths and ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience from start to finish.

Service Area & Availability

We currently provide our full range of services in most parts of Andhra Pradesh. We are actively working on expanding our network to other cities across India to reach more people in need of our support.
